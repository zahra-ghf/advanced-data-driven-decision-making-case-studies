# Advanced Data-Driven Decision Making Case Studies

This repository contains selected case studies completed for the Advanced Data-Driven Decision Making course at GSEM, University of Geneva.

The projects focus on applying data-driven methods to business problems, including experience curve analysis, factor analysis, and structural equation modelling.

## Case Studies

Case Studies
Case Study 1: Heliotronics - Experience Curve Analysis

This case study analyzes manufacturing cost reductions using an experience curve model.
The objective was to estimate the learning effect and understand how cumulative production affects unit cost over time.
The analysis uses regression-based modelling to support pricing and production cost decisions.

Case Study 2: Smartphone Quality Perceptions - Factor Analysis

This case study examines how customers perceive smartphone quality using survey data.
Exploratory Factor Analysis was used to identify the main latent quality dimensions behind customer responses.
The project links customer perception dimensions to business outcomes such as willingness to pay and repurchase intention.

Case Study 3: Galeries Lafayette - Structural Equation Modelling

This case study investigates the drivers of brand equity for Galeries Lafayette Berlin.
The analysis combines Exploratory Factor Analysis, Confirmatory Factor Analysis, and Structural Equation Modelling.
The objective was to understand how customer image perceptions influence satisfaction, affective commitment, repurchase intention, and co-creation intention.
